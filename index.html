About Me

I was once a SE/CS student of China. During my undergraduate years, I conducted rich research on the application of AI models in vegetation of artificial forests such as individual tree modeling and Fine-grained leaf phenotype analysis. Additionally, I have some experiences on land use analysis and crowd monitoring in natural parks of China. Currently, I'm a master student majoring in Geography at Virginia Tech.

My research interests include remote sensing image processing, multi-modal data fusion and interdisciplinary applications between AI and geography.

I am willing to carry out more scientific research cooperation, please email me via: taige@vt.edu.
